VC Subjective Evaluation

Procedure

  1. Enter your name and choose setlist A or B.
  2. Press "Start".
  3. For each sample:
    • Listen to the Target speaker (reference) and the Converted speech.
    • Rate the converted speech on: Naturalness and Speaker Similarity (1–5).
  4. Press "Next" to proceed. On the last page, press "Finish" to download CSV.

Notes

  • Please use headphones.
  • Do not refresh the page during the experiment.
  • This experiment is designed to take about 20–30 minutes.
